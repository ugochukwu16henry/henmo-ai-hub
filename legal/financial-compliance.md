# HenMo AI Financial Compliance Framework

## Legal Structure & Compliance

### 1. Business Registration
- **Entity Type**: Limited Liability Company (LLC) or Corporation
- **Jurisdiction**: Delaware, USA (recommended for tech companies)
- **Tax ID**: Federal EIN required for US operations
- **Nigerian Registration**: CAC registration for local operations

### 2. Financial Licenses & Permits
- **Money Transmitter License**: Required in applicable US states
- **Nigerian Financial License**: CBN approval for payment processing
- **International Compliance**: GDPR, PCI DSS, SOX compliance

### 3. Payment Processor Compliance

#### Stripe Compliance
- ✅ PCI DSS Level 1 certified
- ✅ SOC 2 Type II compliant
- ✅ Supports 195+ countries
- ✅ Automatic tax calculation
- ✅ Built-in fraud protection

#### Paystack Compliance
- ✅ CBN licensed in Nigeria
- ✅ PCI DSS compliant
- ✅ Local bank partnerships
- ✅ Mobile money integration
- ✅ Regulatory reporting

#### Wise Business Compliance
- ✅ FCA regulated (UK)
- ✅ Multi-currency accounts
- ✅ Real exchange rates
- ✅ International transfer compliance
- ✅ AML/KYC procedures

## Financial Record Keeping

### 1. Required Records (7+ years retention)
- All transaction records
- Revenue and expense documentation
- Tax filings and payments
- Employee payroll records
- Contractor payment records
- Bank statements and reconciliations

### 2. Audit Trail Requirements
- Immutable transaction logs
- User action tracking
- Payment processor records
- Currency conversion records
- Refund and chargeback documentation

### 3. Tax Compliance

#### US Tax Obligations
- Federal income tax (21% corporate rate)
- State income tax (varies by state)
- Payroll taxes (Social Security, Medicare, Unemployment)
- Sales tax (varies by state and service type)
- 1099 reporting for contractors ($600+ annually)

#### Nigerian Tax Obligations
- Companies Income Tax (30%)
- Value Added Tax (7.5%)
- Withholding Tax (varies by transaction type)
- Personal Income Tax for employees
- Technology Tax (if applicable)

## Privacy & Data Protection

### 1. Financial Data Privacy
- ✅ Customer payment data encrypted
- ✅ PCI DSS compliance for card data
- ✅ Limited access to financial records
- ✅ Regular security audits
- ✅ Data breach notification procedures

### 2. GDPR Compliance (EU customers)
- Right to access financial data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure (with legal limitations)
- Data portability for transaction history
- Consent management for marketing

### 3. Internal Access Controls
- Role-based access to financial data
- Multi-factor authentication required
- Audit logs for all financial access
- Regular access reviews
- Separation of duties for payments

## Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

### 1. Customer Due Diligence
- Identity verification for high-value transactions
- Source of funds verification
- Ongoing monitoring of customer activity
- Enhanced due diligence for high-risk customers

### 2. Transaction Monitoring
- Automated suspicious activity detection
- Manual review of flagged transactions
- Reporting to relevant authorities (FinCEN, NFIU)
- Record keeping of investigations

### 3. Sanctions Screening
- OFAC sanctions list screening
- EU sanctions compliance
- Nigerian sanctions compliance
- Regular list updates and rescreening

## Financial Reporting Standards

### 1. Internal Reporting
- Daily cash position reports
- Weekly revenue summaries
- Monthly financial statements
- Quarterly board reports
- Annual audited financials

### 2. Regulatory Reporting
- Tax authority filings
- Payment processor reports
- Banking compliance reports
- Securities filings (if applicable)
- International transfer reports

### 3. Stakeholder Reporting
- Investor updates (if applicable)
- Employee financial summaries
- Contributor payout reports
- Customer billing statements

### 1. Financial Risks

- Policy updates and training
